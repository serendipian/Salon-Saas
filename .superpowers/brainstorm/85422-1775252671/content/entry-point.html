Where does plan selection and billing live in the app?

A

Inside Settings → "Abonnement"

A new card in the existing Settings grid. Users click it like any other setting. Simple, no new nav item.

Réglages grid → Abonnement & Facturation card → plan page
B

Upgrade banner throughout the app

A persistent banner at the top when on Free/trial plan. "You are on Free — Upgrade to Pro →". Clicking opens a full-screen plan selector modal.

Banner (free users only) → modal overlay with pricing cards
C

Both: Settings + inline upgrade prompts

Billing lives in Settings (for managing invoices, changing plan). PLUS small "Upgrade" badges on locked features (e.g. analytics tab shows a lock icon). Best discovery but more work to build.

Settings → Abonnement (primary) + feature-level lock icons (secondary)
