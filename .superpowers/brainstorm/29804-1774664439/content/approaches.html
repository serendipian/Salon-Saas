Pros

B

Guided Stepper (Wizard Flow)

Step 1: Pick service + variant → Step 2: Pick staff (shows who can do it) → Step 3: Pick date/time (shows only available slots) → Step 4: Add client + notes → Confirm

Each step filters the next — only valid options shown

Pros

  • Each step narrows options — impossible to pick invalid combos
  • Works great on mobile (one thing at a time)
  • Very guided — less training needed

Cons

  • Slower for experienced receptionists — too many clicks
  • Hard to go back and change earlier steps
  • Can't see everything at once
  • Doesn't match the all-at-once UX you liked in the screenshot
C

Smart Single Column (Enhanced Current)

Keep single-column layout but replace native inputs with inline calendar widget, time chips, and variant pills. Add availability indicators next to staff names.
